Web Development

As a frontend web developer, I specialize in creating digital websites using a range of technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Sass, TypeScript, and React.

Here are some of the services I offer:

  • Website design and development: I can design and build custom websites from scratch, using the latest web design principles and best practices. I can also work with pre-existing designs to bring them to life using HTML, CSS, Sass, JavaScript, TypeScript , React.js and Next.js.
  • Responsive design: I ensure that all of my websites are fully responsive, meaning they look great and function seamlessly on all devices, including smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.
  • User experience (UX) design: I place a strong emphasis on creating intuitive and enjoyable user experiences. I use a variety of techniques, such as user testing and usability testing, to ensure that my websites are easy to navigate and use.
  • Frontend optimization: I understand the importance of fast-loading websites and strive to optimize my designs for speed. This includes techniques like minifying code, optimizing images, and using caching techniques.
  • Maintenance and updates: I offer ongoing support and maintenance for my websites, including regular updates, bug fixes, and feature enhancements.
